About

Crawlr is a 21+ social hub, spotlighting local restuarants and bars, their events and what your community has to say. This web based app helps you explore nearby hot spots effortlessly and safely.

Features

    Real Time Reports:
  • Locals on Crawlr can report how long bar lines are, ride rates and more.
    • Events:
  • Crawlr displays happy hours, live music, bar crawls, and more that restuarants and bars in a community are offering.
    • Social Feed:
  • Users can post their experiances at events in their community
